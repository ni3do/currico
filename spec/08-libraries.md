# Purchase, Download & Libraries

## Overview

Enable purchase and download of individual resources and bundles, with separate personal and school libraries.

## Purchase Flow

1. User selects resource/bundle
2. Completes purchase
3. Purchase recorded for teacher or school account
4. Access granted to full-resolution files

## Library Types

### Personal Library ("My Library")
- Individual teacher's purchased resources
- Accessible only to that teacher

### School Library
- Shared resources purchased with school/team license
- Accessible to all teachers in the school
- Managed by school admin

## Library Page Design

### Item Display
Each item shown as card or row with:
- Title
- Subject
- Quality badge
- Editability badge
- Single primary action button (Download/Open) in distinct primary color

### Design Principles
- Simple card or row format
- Advanced metadata on detail pages (not in list)
- Avoid cluttering library lists

## Preview vs. Owned Content

- Preview files: watermarked or low-resolution
- Full-resolution files: only available after purchase
- Clear visual distinction between preview and owned content
